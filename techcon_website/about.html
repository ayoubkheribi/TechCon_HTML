History

TechCon is an annual event organized by SRC to showcase cutting-edge research in semiconductor and microelectronics technologies. It features presentations by university researchers and students, emphasizing innovation in fields like AI hardware, chip design, and sustainable electronics. The conference fosters collaboration between academia and industry leaders.
